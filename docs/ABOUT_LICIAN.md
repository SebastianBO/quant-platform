## What is Lician?

Lician is a free AI-powered financial data platform that democratizes access to professional-grade stock analysis and investment research.

Think of it as having a financial analyst in your pocket. Ask questions like:
- "Is Apple overvalued right now?"
- "Compare Tesla vs Rivian financials"
- "What's Microsoft's revenue growth trend?"
- "Show me undervalued tech stocks"

Our AI analyzes real financial data and gives you clear, actionable insights.

---

## Key Features

### AI Chat Assistant
Ask any question about stocks in plain English. Get instant answers backed by real financial data, not opinions.

### Real-Time Market Data
Live stock prices, market movers, trading volumes, and price alerts across US and European markets.

### Financial Statements
Complete income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements for comprehensive fundamental analysis.

### AI Valuations
Proprietary fair value estimates using multiple valuation models (DCF, comparables, historical multiples).

### SEC Filings
Direct access to 10-K, 10-Q, 8-K, and other regulatory filings with AI-powered summaries.

### Stock Screener
Filter 100,000+ companies by financials, ratios, sectors, and custom criteria.

### Company Comparisons
Side-by-side comparisons of any two companies across all financial metrics.

### Earnings Analysis
Historical earnings data, surprises, and AI-predicted earnings trends.

### Insider Trading Data
Track what company insiders are buying and selling.

### Institutional Holdings
See which hedge funds and institutions own which stocks.

## Why Free?

We believe financial information should be accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford expensive terminals. Lician is free for basic features, with premium tiers for advanced users who need more.

## Technical Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Supabase, PostgreSQL
- **AI**: Multiple LLMs via Vercel AI SDK (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini)
- **Data**: SEC EDGAR, EODHD, Yahoo Finance APIs
- **Hosting**: Vercel
